Olympic Athletes Overview

This visualization provides an overview of Olympic athletes, showing their age and height. Hover over the points to see more details about each athlete.

  • Age is represented on the X-axis.
  • Height (in cm) is represented on the Y-axis.
  • Hovering over a point reveals the athlete's name, age, height, team, and sport.
  • Points are colored by sport to show the distribution of different sports.

We can observe from the plot that most of the athletes who participated in the Olympic Games are aged between 15 to 35. The common height range is 160 to 190 cm.

Athletes in shooting sport generally have a higher age range when they participated in the Olympic Games, around 35 to 55 years old.

Basketball players who participated in Olympic Games have a height range of 180 to 200 cm.
