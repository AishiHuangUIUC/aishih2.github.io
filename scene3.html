Countries Participation Over the Years

This visualization shows the number of countries that participated in the Olympics each year. Hover over the line to see the number of athletes that attended the Olympics each year.

  • The X-axis represents the year.
  • The Y-axis represents the number of participating countries.
  • Hovering over the line reveals the number of athletes that participated in the corresponding year.
  • The blue line represents summer Olympic Game, the red line represents winter Olympic Game.
